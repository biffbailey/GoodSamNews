LIVE, LOVE, PRAY
Throughout the month of July, we will be studying and reflecting on Evelyn Underhill's masterful book ABBA. Underhill, one of the great experts on Christian mysticism is as profoundly relevant today as when the book was first published. You will be both moved and disturbed by this in-depth encounter into the prayer Our Lord taught his disciples to pray.
