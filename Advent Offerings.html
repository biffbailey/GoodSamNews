Advent Offerings
Offerings take a special meaning each Sunday of Advent. This first Sunday consider:
God is with us in amazing and powerful ways, bringing shining new hope into the world. Like Mary and Elizabeth so long ago, the ministry of our church is radiant with that great hope. And like Mary and Elizabeth, our congregation and the whole Church also trusts in the promises of God. In our giving this morning, our joy overflows in generosity.
Let us gather our gifts together and present them as an offering to God.
