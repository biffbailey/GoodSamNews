Greetings in Christ!
Due to a generous bequest, the Episcopal Diocese of Dallas administers a trust fund granting scholarships to students attending Episcopal schools, including colleges and K-12 schools.
Applications for the Lawrence-Blake scholarship are due March 31.
Scholarships are open to all residents of the diocese, with preference given to students attending St. Mark's School (Southboro, Massachusetts), Trinity College (Hartford, Connecticut), students currently residing in Texarkana, or students who demonstrate financial need.
Please contact Ticoy Young with any questions.
