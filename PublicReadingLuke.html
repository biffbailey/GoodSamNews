1 Timothy 4: 13
Until I arrive, give attention to
the public reading of scripture,
to exhorting, to teaching.
There will be a public reading of the Gospel of Luke on Sunday, February 17, 2019, at 2:00 p.m. at St. Luke's Episcopal Church, Dallas.
This is a unique event where the entire Gospel is publically read aloud utilizing a variety of voices from across the diocese, with the Bishop reading the final chapter.
Please make plans to attend this special event where, as a diocese, we can all be "hearers of the word" together.
