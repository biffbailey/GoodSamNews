Abba Meditations Based on the Lord's Prayer

Please join us in reading and reflecting on Evelyn Underhill's masterful book!

Underhill, one of the great experts on Christian mysticism is as profoundly relevant today as when the book was first published.

You will be both moved and disturbed by this in-depth encounter into the prayer Our Lord taught his disciples to pray.

« We seldom pause for that awed recognition of pure Being , so steadying and refreshing to the soul, which is the raw material of the interior life. »
